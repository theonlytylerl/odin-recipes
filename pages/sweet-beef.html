The Scrumptious Sweet Beef and Rice

Description

This sweet and spicy Korean Ground Beef can be on the table in about 20 minutes, so it is perfect for busy weeknight dinners! Serve it over cooked rice for a tasty dinner the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice, per person
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 TBSP vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 TBSP brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 TBSP lime juice
  • 1-2 TBSP chopped cashews, optional
  • 1 thin sliced carrot, optional

Directions:

  1. Heat garlic in 2 TBSP of vegetable oil until it starts to become aromatic
  2. Add ground beef and break apart. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add sliced carrots; cook until tender
  4. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, sesame oil, ginger powder. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Add to the beef. (can double this sauce.)
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes until sauce is fully absorbed. Stir in green onion. Top with cashews if desired.
