The Heavenly Hawaiian Sweet Rolls

Description

Looking for the perfect anytime appetizer? Look no further than the iconic Ham and Swiss Sliders! These Ham and Cheese Sliders recipe brings you the ultimate combination of irrestistible flavors and buttery goodness. These deliciously sweet and savorit sliders and perfect for Slider Sunday, game day gatherings, summer cookouts, and everything in between.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb deli honey ham
  • 6 slices of swiss cheese, cut into fourths
  • 1 tsp poppy seeds
  • 1 1/2 TBSP dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup butter melted
  • 1 TBSP onion powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 2 packages (12 count) Kings Hawaiian Original Hawaiian Sweet Dinner Rolls

Directions:

  1. Cut rolls in half, set aside tops. Places a slice or two of ham and slice of Swiss cheese on each roll. Replace the top of the rolls and bunch them closely together into a baking dish or cookie pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together poppy seeds, dijon mustard, melted butter, onion powder, and worcestershire sauce.
  3. Pour sauce over the rolls just covering the tops. Cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted. Uncover and serve warm.
